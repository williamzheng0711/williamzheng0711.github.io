About me

I am a PhD student in the Department of Information Engineering at The Chinese University of Hong Kong. My research interests include coding theory, wireless communications, unsourced random access, erasure channels, and learning-based methods for reliable communication systems.

Research

My current work studies communication and coding problems that arise in modern multi-user wireless systems.

  • Safe learning: reinforcement learning methods for safety-critical wireless communications.
  • Learning efficiency: methods to improve the efficiency of learning algorithms in communication systems.
